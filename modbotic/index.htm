ModBotic's Introduction

What is ModBotic?

ModBotic™ is a 100% Customizable Multi-purpose Discord bot that provides you many features to make YOUR life easier in managing your server! You can use it for anything from Advance Moderation & Entertainment to Utility & Logging

ModBotic™ is a 100% free to use Bot mainly so that all users can enjoy the bot to the max without needing to pay for special commands!
